Hello World!

Dec 30, 2020 | 1 minute read
Share this: Twitter Facebook Pinterest Google+

I decided to make this site so I could have a single place on the internet where I can access and share information about myself and things that I’ve worked on. Instead of having to go to my GitHub or my project page, it’s all here now.

How did I make this site?

In all honesty, I didn’t. My goal here isn’t to come up with some fancy website with really cool features (if you’re looking for one of those, check this out) - I just wanted a space on the internet to house my projects, CV, a blog and my contact details. So I just used a premade theme from Hugo called Introduction - I’m a big fan of the minimalist styling. It was really easy to get this site up and running using this template and populate it with my info.

The site is hosted using GitHub pages, which offers free hosting of static sites.

What are my future blog posts going to be about?

Who knows! I’m not expecting many of people to read this, so I’ll probably just be filling it with articles about things that I’m interested in/learning about in my free time.

If you made it this far, thanks for reading :)
